PET Only

Colors show the intensity of the PET image. The original intensity range of the PET image is 0-65535 (16-bit); the range 0-8192 is selected for the visualization.

PET+CT

Volume renderings of the PET image and the CT image are combined. Colors show the intensity of the PET image. The original intensity range of the PET image is 0-65535 (16-bit); the range 0-8192 is selected for the visualization. Bones from the CT image can provide locality information.

PET-filtered CT

The CT image is filtered by the PET image. Only the regions with high PET intensity values are shown opaquely. The plot at bottom-left shows the filtering function used; the x-axis is the PET image value while the y-axis represents opacity.
